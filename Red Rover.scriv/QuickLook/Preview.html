Elva Wakes Up
Elva wakes up in the kitchen, realizes she’s been knocked out by cleaning fluid vapors, uses the stove exhaust to clear the air, and exits the kitchen to explore the ship. Some background and exposition through her inventory, and the locket.

Elva explores
Elva makers her way towards the bridge, but damages some systems while doing so. She also finds and disables a sabotaged system, dropping the ship out of Casimir drive. The transition knocks her out.

Rover wakes up
Rover wakes up in his pod, disoriented. He can explore around the command center a bit. When he wakes Janet up, she recognizes the system failure. She trips over Elva’s body just outside the bridge door, ties Elva up, and tells Rover to keep guard.

Rover in the middle
Rover can run back and forth between Elva (command center) and Janet (engineering). He puts together what’s going on, but can’t fully express it to either. The solution is for him to bring Elva and Janet together one way or another.

Janet Explores
Janet leaves Rover in charge, tells Elva not to touch anything. Janet finds traces of past civilization, but can’t translate. She is trapped, and uses ansible to call for help. Now it is a race against time before Lysithians show up.

The Rescue
Elva jumps Valkyrie into the planet. Rover translates the artifacts, and they understand what’s going on. The underground base powers up as the Lysithians approach.

Ending
The Valkyrie is embedded too deeply in the planet to get out. Solutions? Self-destruct? Ask Lysithians for Mercy? Connect planetary power to Valkyrie and Casimir jump the entire planet?
